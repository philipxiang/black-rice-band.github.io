About the Band

Black Rice is St. John's School's premier hard rock band, bringing explosive energy and classic rock vibes to Houston's middle school music scene.

Influenced by legendary bands like Def Leppard and KISS, we deliver powerful performances that blend technical skill with raw rock 'n' roll attitude.

Our mission is simple: to rock hard, honor our school, and inspire the next generation of rock musicians.
